Resize Image to 20KB Online

Use the Custom preset in Resizer India to set your own dimensions and compress JPG output under 20KB for online forms and portals.

Open the custom 20KB image resizer

When this is useful

  • Government forms asking for photo under 20KB.
  • Signature uploads with strict KB limits.
  • Job portal profile photos.
  • College, exam and document upload portals.

How to compress to 20KB

  1. Open Custom mode.
  2. Enter the required width and height.
  3. Set target max size to 20KB.
  4. Upload, crop and download the JPG file.

Why exact KB can vary

JPG size depends on the image content. Simple backgrounds compress easily, while detailed photos may need lower quality or smaller dimensions to fit under the same KB limit.
