Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Resizer India is designed to process images in your browser. When you use the current resize tool, your selected image is handled locally by your browser and is not uploaded to a Resizer India server.

Information we process

The tool reads the image you select only for resizing, cropping and compression inside your browser. The output file is created on your device for download.

Analytics and ads

Resizer India may use privacy-respecting analytics or advertising services in the future to understand traffic and support the free tool. If ads or analytics are added, this policy should be updated with the exact services used.

Your responsibility

Avoid uploading sensitive documents to any online tool unless you trust how it works. Resizer India aims to keep image processing local wherever possible.
