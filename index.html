How to resize a photo for driving licence forms

  1. Choose RTO Photo or RTO Signature.
  2. Upload your image from your phone or computer.
  3. Adjust the crop with zoom and position controls.
  4. Resize and download the JPG file.
  5. Upload the downloaded file on the form portal.

Common upload rejection reasons

Wrong dimensions

Many portals check exact pixel width and height. Use the preset instead of resizing manually.

File size too high

If the site asks for 20KB, a 21KB file can fail. This tool compresses JPG output under the limit.

Unclear photo or signature

Use a bright, plain background and avoid shadows, ruled paper or blurry camera photos.

FAQs

Are my photos uploaded to Resizer India?

No. The current tool works in your browser using canvas, so the image stays on your device.

What format does the tool download?

The download is JPG because most government and form portals accept JPG or JPEG image uploads.

Can I use it for non-RTO forms?

Yes. Use Custom mode to set the width, height and target KB required by another form.
