Driving Licence Photo Resizer 420x525

Use Resizer India to prepare a driving licence or RTO photo in 420 x 525 px JPG format with a target size between 10KB and 20KB.

Open the RTO photo resizer

RTO photo requirements

  • Preferred dimensions: 420 x 525 px.
  • Output format: JPG or JPEG.
  • Common file size range: 10KB to 20KB.
  • Use a clear passport-style photo with a light background.

How to use

  1. Open the RTO photo preset.
  2. Upload your photo.
  3. Adjust zoom and position until the face is clear.
  4. Resize and download the JPG file.

Tips to avoid rejection

Avoid dark backgrounds, blurry photos, sunglasses, caps covering the face and images with too much empty space around the face. Always verify the latest official portal instructions before final submission.
