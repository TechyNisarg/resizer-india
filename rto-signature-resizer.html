Signature requirements

  • Output dimensions: 256 x 64 px.
  • Output format: JPG or JPEG.
  • Common file size range: 10KB to 20KB.
  • Use black or blue ink on clean white paper.

How to prepare a signature image

  1. Sign on plain white paper.
  2. Take a clear photo in bright light or scan it.
  3. Upload the image and choose the RTO Signature preset.
  4. Adjust the crop so the signature fills the frame clearly.

Common mistakes

Avoid ruled notebook paper, very thin pencil marks, shadows and blurry camera images. A clean high-contrast signature usually compresses better and remains readable.
