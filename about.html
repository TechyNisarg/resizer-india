About

Why Resizer India exists

Resizer India started from a simple real-life problem: online forms often reject photos, signatures and documents because the image has the wrong dimensions or file size.

The goal is to make a practical Indian form utility platform where people can quickly prepare upload-ready files for driving licence, exam, job, bank and document portals.

What the tool does

  • Resize images to exact pixel dimensions.
  • Compress JPG output under a target KB limit.
  • Help with photo and signature upload requirements.
  • Process images in the browser where possible.

What comes next

More dedicated tools are planned for SSC, UPSC, RRB, banking exams, PAN card, passport photo, image compression and PDF preparation.
